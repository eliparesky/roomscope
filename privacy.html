Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RoomScope ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information across our services, including the RoomScope mobile application (the "App") and the RoomScope website (the "Site"), together referred to as the "Service." By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

1. Who We Are

RoomScope is a service that creates detailed 3D models of college dorm rooms and helps incoming students visualize, customize, and furnish those rooms before move-in. The Service is operated by Eli Paresky (sole proprietor), based in Durham, North Carolina, United States. You can contact us at privacy@roomscope.ai.

2. Who Uses the Service

The Service has two main types of users:

  • Scanners who use the App to capture LiDAR scans of dorm rooms and submit them to RoomScope.
  • Consumers ("Students" or "Shoppers") who use the Site to view 3D models of their assigned or prospective dorm rooms, customize furniture layouts, and purchase items through our retail partners.

Some individuals may be both Scanners and Consumers. This Policy covers both uses.

3. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password. Passwords are never stored in plain text; they are handled by Firebase Authentication, which uses industry-standard cryptographic hashing. We may also collect your name, school, and graduation year.

b. Scan Data (from Scanners)

When a Scanner submits a scan through the App, we collect:

  • The 3D scan file produced by Apple's RoomPlan framework (including room dimensions and detected objects like beds, desks, and doors)
  • Photos of the room captured during or after scanning
  • Metadata you provide or that is automatically recorded, including building name, room number, school, scan duration, and the date and time of the scan
  • The identifier of the Scanner account that submitted the scan

Scanners are responsible for ensuring that every room they scan is a space they are permitted to enter and scan, and that scans do not capture identifiable images of other people without those people's consent. See our Terms of Service for details.

c. Shopping and Customization Data (from Consumers)

When a Consumer uses the Site, we collect the room models they view, the customizations they make (for example, which furniture pieces they add or swap), items they save or share, and clicks on affiliate links. We do not process payments ourselves; all purchases are completed through our retail partners, who handle payment information under their own privacy policies.

d. Device and Usage Information

We collect limited technical information to operate the Service and diagnose problems, including device model, operating system version, App or browser version, crash logs, IP address (truncated for analytics), and a device token used to deliver push notifications through the App.

e. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect your precise GPS location, your contacts, your microphone audio, your browsing history outside our Service, or any health or financial information. We do not track you across other apps or websites for advertising purposes.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow Scanners to submit scans and review their own submission history
  • Review, approve, or request retakes of submitted scans
  • Build and display 3D room models to Consumers who are considering or assigned to a given room
  • Automatically generate suggested furniture layouts using our retail catalog
  • Enable Consumers to customize room layouts and purchase products through our retail partners
  • Send push notifications and emails related to your account (such as retake requests for Scanners or order updates for Consumers)
  • Track affiliate sales attributed to your Consumer account, for purposes of operating our revenue model
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. How Information Is Stored and Shared

a. Storage

Scan data, account information, shopping history, and related metadata are stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage), a service provided by Google LLC. Data is protected by Firebase's security rules, which restrict access based on account role.

b. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers that host or operate the Service on our behalf, including Google Firebase (storage, authentication, cloud functions), Apple (push notification delivery via APNs), and any analytics or error-monitoring tools we may integrate in the future
  • Retail partners that fulfill Consumer orders. When you click an affiliate link and complete a purchase, the retailer receives the information they need to fulfill your order, under their own privacy policies. We may receive reports from retail partners indicating that a purchase was made.
  • Other users of the Service, in limited ways: Consumers may see the 3D model of a dorm room, but not the identity of the Scanner who submitted it. Scanners may see information about their own submissions but not about other Scanners' submissions.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect our rights, safety, or property, or the rights, safety, or property of others

We do not share your individual email address, password, or account credentials with other users.

c. Aggregated and Anonymized Data

We may use scan data, usage data, and other information in aggregated or de-identified form to create room models, train algorithms that improve our service, generate market insights, and publish statistics. Aggregated and de-identified data is not subject to the sharing limits described above.

d. International Transfers

Our service providers may process data in the United States or other countries. By using the Service, you consent to this processing.

6. Data Retention

We retain account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account at any time by emailing privacy@roomscope.ai. Scan data submitted by Scanners is part of our Service's content library and may be retained after a Scanner's account is deleted. Where we retain scan data after account deletion, it is disassociated from personal account identifiers where technically feasible. Consumer purchase records may be retained for as long as required by tax, accounting, or other legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email privacy@roomscope.ai. We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with applicable law.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information to us, please contact us and we will delete it. Because the Service is designed for college students and incoming college students, most users will be at least 17.

9. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), authenticated access, and database security rules that restrict who can read and write data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. If changes are material, we will provide additional notice through the Service or by email.
